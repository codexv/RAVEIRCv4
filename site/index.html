Built for operators

Everything the original RAVE script did, rebuilt natively — plus a real scripting engine under the hood.

Channel protection

Per-channel protections you can configure offline — flood, ban, and kick handling straight from the Channel Manager.

Real mSL scripting

A built-in mIRC-compatible scripting engine — aliases, events, identifiers, hash tables, timers, and a script editor in its own window.

Cross-platform & native

One codebase, native builds for macOS (universal), Windows, and Linux — light on memory, instant to launch.

Auto-updates

Updates download and apply in-app — no reinstalling. Signed release artifacts verified before they're applied.

Keychain security

NickServ passwords are stored in your OS keychain — encrypted at rest on every platform, never in plaintext.

Stays connected

Keepalive and auto-reconnect with backoff keep you online — without ever resetting your whois idle streak.
